🔌 Ingress Type *

Select how users will access the Amazon Business account. This determines which email templates are generated.

📧

Direct Access

Users receive an invitation email from business@amazon.com and register directly on Amazon Business.

🔗

Punchout

Users access Amazon Business through a tile or link in their eProcurement system (Coupa, Ariba, etc.).

🔒

UIES + Sharable Link

Email invitation is suppressed. Users register via a Sharable Link provided by the company. Includes required legal disclaimer.

🔐

Punchout + SSO (UIES)

Punchout access with SSO/IDP authentication. Account auto-created; users access via ePRO system.
